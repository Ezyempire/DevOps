Ghana's Economic Structure

Ghana, located in West Africa, has a diverse economic structure. The country's economy is characterized by agriculture, mining, and services. Cocoa production, gold mining, and oil exports are significant contributors to Ghana's GDP. The government has been implementing policies to promote sustainable development and attract foreign investment in various sectors.

  • Joseph Arthur Ankrah (1966 - 1969)
  • Akuafo Addo (1969)
  • Ignatius Kutu Acheampong (1972 - 1978)
  • Dr. Hilla Limann (1979 - 1981)
  • Jerry John Rawlings (1981 - 2001)
  • John Agyekum Kufuor (2001 - 2009)
  • John Evans Atta Mills (2009 - 2012)
  • John Dramani Mahama (2012 - 2017)
  • Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo (2017 - Present)
